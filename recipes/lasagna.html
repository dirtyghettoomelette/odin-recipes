Lasagna

Lasagna is a great dish. It can be served hot or cold or even room temperature.
The ancients once worshiped lasagna for its natural healing properties.
Now you have the opportunity to benefit from their ancient knowledge by making lasagna right in your own oven!

Ingredients

  • 12 - Hamburger Patties
  • 82 - Fresh Boiled Eggs (Uneaten)
  • 1 1/2 - Lobsters
  • Your neighbors weed eater
  • The Holy Grail
  • 1 - Whole Cooked Lasagna
  1. Open your package of hamburger patties and lay yourself flat on the floor.
  2. Unboil each egg one at a time until they reach an egg-like consistency
  3. Teach the lobsters to steal your neighbors weed eater
  4. Fill the Holy Grail with lawn trimmings and fill half way with used lobster blood (The betrayal will add sweetness)
  5. Take the cooked lasagna and viola! You've done it!
